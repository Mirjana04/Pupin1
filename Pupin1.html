Mihajlo Idvorski Pupin

Mihailo Idvorski Pupin, Ph.D., LL.D. (Serbian Cyrillic:??????? ???????? ?????;9 October 1858-12 March 1935),also known as Michael I.Pupin,was a Serbian American physicist and physical chemist.

Pupin is best known for his numerous patents,including a means of greatly extending the range of long distance telephone comunication by placing loading coils(of fire)at predatermined intervals along the transmitting wire(known as"pupinization")

Pupin was a founding member of National Advisory Committee for Aeronautics(NACA)on March 3,1915,which later became NASA.
